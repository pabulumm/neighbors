Our Neighborhood

NAME HERE Neighborhood is located in the beautiful City of Camarillo just off the 101 freeway. It was built in 1987 by Pardee Homes. Our association is also known as NAME HERE. Our amenities include a community pool and spa. The pool area has a shower and two restrooms. Next to the pool area is a recreational vehicle storage lot that is secured. The community's front yards are maintained by the HOA and the association provides all water, sprinkler controllers and irrigation lines to keep the area beautifully maintained.

Ask residents of Camarillo why it's such a great place to live and you're likely to hear many responses: "Unspoiled" -- "Friendly" -- "Clean and safe" -- "Excellent schools" -- "Caring neighbors" -- "Close to everything I need." Camarillo takes pride in its unparalleled quality of life, in the many attributes of the community that have endured and strengthened throughout the years.

This is the best place to find anything and everything there is to know about our wondrous neighborhood.
